Some tips to improve your Cooking Style:

1. Clean as you go.
2. Embrace salt. Don’t be afraid to use salt; it pulls the flavors out of your dishes. Cook with kosher salt and season with sea salt.
3. No luck finding shallots? Replace with a combination of onions and garlic.
4. After handling garlic, rub your fingers on stainless steel, like your sink, to get rid of the odor.
5. Prepping salad before serving is a huge time saver. Layer all the ingredients in a bowl and don’t add the dressing until it's time to serve.
6. Keep your spices away from sources of heat like the stove or lights. Herbs and spices can lose their flavor when exposed to humidity and heat.
7. Save old, stale bread to make breadcrumbs in a food processor; you can freeze them for up to 6 months.
8. Let steaks come to room temperature before seasoning and grilling.
